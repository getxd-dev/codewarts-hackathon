# Bayanihan Bridge PH Pitch Script

## Hook

In the Philippines, talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. A student may have a certificate, a handwritten school record, or a simple resume, yet still not know which job, course, or scholarship to pursue next.

## Challenge

Opportunity inequality is not only about motivation. It is shaped by education gaps, device access, internet access, income barriers, location, and unclear pathways from school to work.

## Solution

Bayanihan Bridge PH is an AI-powered opportunity navigator. It asks for a short profile, reads an uploaded document through OCR or mock OCR, calculates an Opportunity Score, and recommends jobs, free courses, scholarships, missing skills, and the next three steps.

## Demo

For the demo, we complete a profile, upload a resume or certificate, extract readable text, and generate a pathway. The result shows a score like 58 out of 100, matched beginner roles such as Data Encoder or Virtual Assistant Trainee, missing skills like Excel or interview readiness, and practical next steps.

## Impact

The app aligns with SDG 4 for education, SDG 8 for decent work, SDG 10 for reduced inequalities, and SDG 11 for stronger communities. It turns scattered documents and social barriers into a clear action plan.

## Vision

The next phase connects verified local opportunities from schools, LGUs, NGOs, and employers, adds real OCR, supports Filipino and regional languages, and helps communities guide more learners into dignified education and work pathways.
