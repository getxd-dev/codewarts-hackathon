# OportuniPH Pitch Script

## Hook

In the Philippines, talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. A student or fresh graduate may have a simple resume, yet still not know which job, course, or scholarship to pursue next.

## Challenge

Opportunity inequality is not only about motivation. It is shaped by education gaps, device access, internet access, income barriers, location, and unclear pathways from school to work.

## Solution

OportuniPH is an AI-powered talent marketplace for Filipino pathways. It asks for a short profile, analyzes an uploaded resume or generates one with a camera-captured applicant photo, calculates an Opportunity Score, and recommends jobs, available courses, scholarships or support, missing skills, and the next three steps.

## Demo

For the demo, we start by choosing Applicant, complete a profile, generate a resume from the profile, open the camera, wait for the readiness checks, capture a photo, and generate a pathway. Then we return to Home, choose Employer, create a job offer, and show it appearing in the Market page. The result is a two-sided flow for candidates and Filipino employers.

## Impact

The app aligns with SDG 4 for education, SDG 8 for decent work, SDG 10 for reduced inequalities, and SDG 11 for stronger communities. It turns scattered documents and social barriers into a clear action plan.

## Vision

The next phase connects verified local opportunities from schools, LGUs, NGOs, and employers, moves Gemini analysis into a production backend, supports Filipino and regional languages, and helps communities guide more learners into dignified education and work pathways.
